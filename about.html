About
Parul University

Established in 2009, Parul University is a leading private university in Gujarat, India, committed to providing world-class education and fostering innovation across diverse academic disciplines.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, foster research and innovation, and develop skilled professionals who contribute to society and the global economy.

Our Vision

To be a world-class university recognized for academic excellence, innovative research, and holistic development of students.

Our Values

Excellence in Education, Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, Social Responsibility, and Commitment to sustainable development.

Our Rich History

2009 - Foundation

Parul University was established as a private university in Gujarat with a vision for excellence.

2012 - Medical College

Launched Parul Institute of Medical Sciences & Research with state-of-the-art facilities.

2016 - University Status

Granted university status by UGC, expanding academic programs and research initiatives.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital learning infrastructure and smart campus initiatives.
